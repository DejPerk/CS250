# CS250
How do I interpret user needs and implement them into a program? How does creating “user stories” help with this?

A way to interpret user needs is by ensuring the communication between you and the users is clear 
and concise and all the stories are laid out so that they can be put in an order based on their importance. 
The user stories are an enormous value to the product's creation and that's how the applications are made and high quality.

How do I approach developing programs? What agile processes do I hope to incorporate into my future development work?

When I approach a new program I want to do my best to have an agile mindset determining which stories 
to prioritize over others and who will be in charge of what pieces so that the product is of good quality. 
Implementing agile also can lead a team to substantial communication, effective production and will 
promote consistent improvement.    

What does it mean to be a good team member in software development?

I think to be a good team player in software development you have to embrace each agile methodology 
and ensure that each team member is understanding the goals through each story.
Making sure that the different team members are honest and transparent to show that they are 
either confused, lost or can lend a hand to aid their other members. 
This way the group is able to produce a high quality product that meets the criteria of the customer.
